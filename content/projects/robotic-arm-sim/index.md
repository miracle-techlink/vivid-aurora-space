A joint research project between Tongji University's Sino-German Industry 4.0 Lab and Siemens, replacing legacy particle swarm optimization (PSO) with deep reinforcement learning for higher-precision robotic arm control.

**Technical work:**
- **DQN agent** for joint trajectory optimization, replacing PSO baseline
- **Three.js 3D visualization** — interactive slider-based parameter control, real-time model updates, importable industrial equipment scenes
- **SITP project certification** completed

**Key insight:** After exposure to NVIDIA Isaac Sim, identified that the core need is not model import/visualization but custom algorithm interfaces and GPU-accelerated physics simulation — pivoted research direction accordingly.
